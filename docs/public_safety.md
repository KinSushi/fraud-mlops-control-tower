# Public Safety

## Allowed

- synthetic data;
- generated model artifacts;
- public technical documentation;
- model cards and data cards;
- local runbooks.

## Forbidden

- real banking data;
- real insurance or health data;
- real client data;
- employer data;
- CVs and applications;
- secrets and tokens;
- production decisioning claims;
- financial, insurance, health or legal advice.
